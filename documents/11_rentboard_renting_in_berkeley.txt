Renting in Berkeley: Key Information

Tenant Screening and Application Fees

Landlords may charge screening or application fees with limits. "Prospective tenants should receive a copy of their credit report if the landlord obtained one, a receipt showing the cost of the screening, and a refund of any unused portion of the fee."

Before Signing a Lease

Affordability: Fixed-term leases obligate you to pay rent for the entire term. Breaking a lease early may make you liable for remaining rent if the landlord cannot re-rent the unit.

Unit Coverage: Most Berkeley rental units receive some rent control protections, though some are exempt. Check which protections apply to your specific unit.

Key lease terms to clarify:
- How "last month's rent" will be applied
- Whether discounted rent affects future increases
- Utility payment responsibilities—for tenancies starting February 6, 2024 or later, landlords may only charge utilities if they're part of base rent or separately metered with the tenant's name on the account

Documentation: Obtain a signed, written lease copy and receipts for all payments.

Notice of Tenant Rights

For tenancies beginning December 20, 2024 or later, landlords must provide written notice within 15 days explaining rent control protections, exemptions, and your right to petition against certain increases.

Moving In

Document the unit's condition with photos, written descriptions, and witness observations to protect your security deposit.

Lease Expiration and Renewal

The lease expiration itself is not grounds for eviction. As of November 2024, you may refuse to sign a new fixed-term lease; the tenancy converts to month-to-month under original terms once rent is accepted.

Moving Out

Provide 30 days' written notice to terminate a month-to-month tenancy.

Additional notes for Cal students (per Berkeley Rent Board guidance): Students have the right to petition to reduce rent due to poor housing conditions; landlords cannot tell you to leave just because they don't like you or want to sell the property; and you have the right to interest on your security deposit. Landlords can request up to two months' rent for unfurnished units or three times the rent for furnished units, though as of July 1, 2024 many landlords may only charge one month's rent. Any remaining security deposit must be returned within 21 days after the tenant vacates, with a written itemized statement for any deductions. Contact the Berkeley Rent Board at (510) 981-7368.
